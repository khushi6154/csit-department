About Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya

Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya (GGV) is a central university located in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, India. Established on June 16, 1983, it is one of the largest and oldest institutions of higher education in the state. The university is named after the revered Satnami Guru Ghasidas, who was instrumental in promoting social equality and truth.

GGV offers a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines. The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is an active member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU). The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has accredited the university with an 'A++' grade, reflecting its commitment to academic excellence.

The sprawling campus spans approximately 655 acres and is situated about five kilometers from the main Bilaspur town, with the serene Arpa River flowing nearby. The university boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, including a central library with over 105,000 books, modern laboratories, auditoriums, hostels, and sports facilities.

About the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) at GGV is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering research in the fields of computer science and IT. Our mission is to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

We offer a range of programs, including Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering, Master of Technology (M.Tech), and doctoral programs. Our curriculum is designed to cover both foundational and advanced topics, ensuring that students are well-prepared for both industry roles and academic pursuits.

The department is home to experienced faculty members who are actively engaged in research and development. Our research areas encompass artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, and more. We encourage students to participate in research projects, internships, and workshops to enhance their practical skills.

Facilities within the department include modern computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software, a dedicated library section, and collaborative spaces for project work. We also have active student clubs and societies that organize events, hackathons, and seminars, providing students with opportunities to learn beyond the classroom.

Our alumni have gone on to make significant contributions in various sectors, including technology, academia, and entrepreneurship. We take pride in our strong industry connections, which facilitate internships, placements, and collaborative projects.
