About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) at Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya aims to provide quality education and research opportunities in the field of computing and information sciences. We are committed to nurturing skilled professionals equipped with technical expertise and innovative thinking to meet the evolving demands of the tech industry.
